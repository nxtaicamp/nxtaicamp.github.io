    Tell me more

    We strongly believe that the future of our world lies in STEM, and our first courses represent the changes happening now. We currently have 4 courses:

    1. Beginner Python: Introduces students to STEM with Python, a foundational language that is easy to learn and even easier to start building the future with
    2. Advanced Python: Meant for students with a grasp on Python to start learning more advanced features of the language and building web, desktop, and mobile apps
    3. Introduction to Artificial Intelligence: Introduces students to AI with a simple pathway. Discusses popular AI frameworks and goes deeper than other courses and talks about the why and not just the how of how models work
    4. Applications in Artificial Intelligence: For students who know some AI, introduces them into where AI can be applied and helps them build their own AI applications

    Who are you guys?

    Our mentors are well-versed in Python and AI, and have extensive knowledge in both areas.
