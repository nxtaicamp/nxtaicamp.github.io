We are different

We strongly believe that the future of our world lies in AI and the first steps to the future start today. Our students learn the basics of AI, from the initial theory, all the way to mastery, where they will have the skills to implement their own projects.

NXT AI students start their first two weeks delving deep into AI, learning Python, advanced data science, algorithms, and the fundements of AI. Spread across the two weeks, students will complete small projects with what they learn to ensure deep understanding and have access to around-the-clock mentors who can guide them through problems.

Following that, they will get to spend the next week creating their own AI projects, with guidance from our mentors, to ensure success and build transferable skills to other domains of interest and present them at our camp-wide expo, where projects compete for a prize.



Our team and beliefs

Our mentors go through a rigorous process to pick out those who are best equipped to teach students and offer guidance to any questions they have. We strongly believe that the most important part of learning is a good mentor, someone to guide students when they hit roadblocks, and we never had those -- therefore, NXT AI hopes to offer these to students, completely free of charge.

Many of our mentors have real-world experience in AI, ranging from research at organizations, internships at FAANG companies, and foundings of successful startups, ensuring that they know end-to-end what skills students need to flourish.
